Vacaville, USA

Location

Vacaville is located in northern California, about 1-2 hours from San Francisco.

About

Weather

Vacaville is warm most of the year, but can get chilly in the mornings due to the bay area mist coming in from San Francisco.

Population

About 103,000 people call Vacaville home, making it a lively and fun community.

Sports

Baseball and basketball are especially popular here, with local teams drawing big crowds.
